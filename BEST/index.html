Basin Employment Service and Training, Inc. prides itself in offering personalized employment service, molded to fit the needs of our clients, both employers and employees. Whether it be part-time, full-time, temporary or seasonal employment, Basin Employment strives to be the "BEST".


B.E.S.T. specializes in High-tech and professional job placement, and our employee base includes general laborers, clerical workers, engineers, technicians, skilled craftsmen, farm workers and everything in between.


Basin Employment also provides specialized training and testing, and a Certified Forklift Facilitator is on staff for those in the industrial and agricultural fields.

Job placement for the entry level, industrial, and the professional employment seeker.
